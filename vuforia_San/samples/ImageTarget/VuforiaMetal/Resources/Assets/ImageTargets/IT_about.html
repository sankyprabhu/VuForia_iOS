This sample shows how to use Vuforia with the Metal Framework.

Key Functionality

  • Detection and tracking of an Image Target
  • BindVideoBackground toggle for enabling processing of the video background

Targets

Stones and Chips

Instructions

  • Point camera at target to view 3D augmentation
  • Double tap to access options menu
  • Use toggle to enable BindVideoBackground
